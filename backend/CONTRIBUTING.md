### 1. Fork the Repository

### 2. Clone Your Fork

**Using the command line:**
```bash

````

Replace `<your-username>` and `<repository-name>` with your information.

1. Create a new branch for your work:


3. Test your changes to ensure everything works correctly.

## üîÄ Submitting a Pull Request (PR)

   * Describe your changes and why you made them.
   * Tag reviewers if needed.

3. Be open to feedback ‚Äî maintainers may request tweaks before merging.
